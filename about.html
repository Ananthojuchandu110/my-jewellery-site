A Legacy of Artistry

Established in 1928, Sri Surya Chandra Jewels has been a guardian of Indian heritage. Our designs are inspired by the celestial dance of the Sun and the Moon—symbols of eternal radiance and serenity.

Every piece we create is more than just jewellery; it is a modern heirloom, crafted with 22K BIS Hallmarked gold and a commitment to transparency.
